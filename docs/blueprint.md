# **App Name**: ClosetMind

## Core Features:

- Wardrobe Catalog: Users can upload photos of their clothing items, categorize them (e.g., tops, bottoms, accessories), and add details like color, brand, and occasion. This forms the user's digital wardrobe.
- Outfit Assembler: Users can visually select and combine items from their catalog to create, save, and manage custom outfits.
- AI Outfit Recommender: A generative AI tool that suggests complete outfit combinations from the user's catalog based on selected criteria such as occasion, weather, or specific garment inclusions.
- Outfit Planner: Integrate a calendar view where users can schedule their saved outfits for future dates, assisting with daily planning.
- Weather-Aware Suggestions: The AI tool can consider current and forecast weather conditions for the user's location when generating outfit recommendations.

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary color: Muted lavender (#A38FB6) to convey a sense of calm creativity and refined elegance. HSL: 270, 30%, 60%.
- Background color: A very light, desaturated grey-lavender (#F2F0F5) that feels clean and modern, allowing wardrobe items to stand out. HSL: 270, 15%, 96%.
- Accent color: A sophisticated blue-violet (#4040BF) for interactive elements and highlights, providing clear contrast against the primary and background. HSL: 240, 50%, 50%.
- Headline font: 'Belleza' (humanist sans-serif) for an artistic, fashion-forward, and modern aesthetic in titles and key messaging.
- Body font: 'Alegreya' (humanist serif) for elegant, highly readable, and contemporary body text and item descriptions.
- Utilize clean, minimalist line icons throughout the interface to maintain a sleek and organized visual language consistent with fashion and technology.
- Employ a grid-based layout for displaying wardrobe items and outfits, facilitating easy browsing and management. Utilize card-style elements for item details to ensure clarity and visual appeal.
- Incorporate subtle and smooth transition animations when adding new items, switching between views, or generating outfit suggestions, enhancing the user experience without distraction.
